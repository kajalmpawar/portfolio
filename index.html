About

Kajal

Originally from Mumbai, India - Kajal grew up triligual. Having heard stories in different languages, her admiration for storytelling comes from her love for languages. Kajal is a Journalist and an Interactive Media Specialist but she also take on the roles of an anchor, photographer, videographer and graphic designer as per the requirements of the project.

She has a bachelor degree in Mass Media from Mumbai University and a post-graduate certficate in Journalism from Humber College, Toronto. She is currently enrolled in the post-graduate Interactive Media Management program at Algonquin College.
